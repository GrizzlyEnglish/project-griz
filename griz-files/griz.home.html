About Me

I never know what to say in these things. I'm just a guy like every other guy. I guess the thing that really sets me apart from everyone else? My love for programming. Nothing beats putting on some headphones and solving giant problems one step at time, or playing a detective and walking through a ton of logic to find that one tiny bug where we misattributed an identifier.
I started out as a self taught programmer. I got a job at a mattress store and had many hours in the day where I just held the store front waiting for a customer to enter the store; so, I picked up programming. What started out as making pong and brick breaker, quickly turned into me fixing a business problem by creating a delivery tracker for a small mattress business to keep track of their daily deliveries and customer statisfaction.
I have since returned to the University of Louisville to persue my degree in Computer Information Systems, and currently am set to graduate May of 2019. I also obtained a job as a Junior Web developer working in .NET.
My plan for the future? Once I graduate I want to move to game development on the side. I have many, many projects that are unfished because school work came first, so once I finish school I will begin making games again. Maybe I can make something cool.
